# Economic Anxiety Index (EAI)

## Definition

Economic Anxiety Index measures the level of public economic concern and uncertainty caused by:

- inflation
- unemployment
- economic crisis narratives
- public fear
- media pressure
- policy uncertainty

---

## Main Dimensions

### 1. Economic Sentiment
Measures public economic optimism and pessimism.

Indicators:
- negative economic comments
- fear sentiment
- uncertainty keywords

---

### 2. Media Pressure
Measures intensity of economic crisis narratives in media.

Indicators:
- inflation headlines
- recession narratives
- panic-related news

---

### 3. Public Emotional Response
Measures emotional reactions toward economic conditions.

Indicators:
- fear
- anxiety
- anger
- distrust

---

### 4. Behavioral Signals
Measures behavioral response to economic uncertainty.

Indicators:
- panic buying
- spending reduction
- investment hesitation

---

## Potential Data Sources

- news portals
- social media
- YouTube comments
- economic policy statements
- public discussions

---

## Expected Output

- Economic Anxiety Score
- Public Fear Level
